About Us

Calumpijan Island, nestled in the picturesque province of Leyte in the Philippines, offers visitors a serene and unspoiled beach experience. Unlike the more commercialized destinations, this island boasts a relatively short yet stunning sandy shore that glistens under the tropical sun, with its sand exhibiting a unique blend of white and golden hues. Calumpijan Island is the perfect destination for those seeking a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life. Its isolation provides a sense of serenity, allowing you to reconnect with nature and find solace in its untouched beauty. The island offers a unique opportunity to unwind and recharge, surrounded by the gentle sounds of the ocean and the calming breeze.
